Virtual function, which computes the wall shear stress and heat flux from the data at the exchange location.

Parameters
[in]tExchange- Temperature at the exchange location.
[in]velExchange- Velocity at the exchange location.
[in]muExchange- Laminar viscosity at the exchange location.
[in]pExchange- Pressure at the exchange location.
[in]Wall_HeatFlux- Value of the wall heat flux, if prescribed.
[in]HeatFlux_Prescribed- Whether or not the wall heat flux is prescribed.
[in]Wall_Temperature- Value of the wall temperature, if prescribed.
[in]Temperature_Prescribed- Wheter or not the wall temperature is prescribed.
[in]FluidModel- Fluid model used in the solver.
[out]tauWall- Wall shear stress, to be computed.
[out]qWall- Wall hear flux, to be computed (if not prescribed).
[out]ViscosityWall- Laminar viscosity at the wall, to be computed.
[out]OverCvWall- Thermal conductivity divided by Cv at the wall, to be computed.

Reimplemented in CWallModelLogLaw, and CWallModel1DEQ.
